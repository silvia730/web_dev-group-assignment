Educational Resources

Understanding the risks and effects of drug abuse is crucial to prevention. Here are some important resources:

Drugs and Their Effects
Drug Category Effects
Heroin Opioids Highly addictive, causes euphoria, drowsiness, and slows breathing.
Cocaine Stimulants Increases heart rate, energy, and causes paranoia.
Marijuana Cannabinoids Relaxation, altered senses, and impaired motor skills.
Ecstasy (MDMA) Hallucinogens Increased empathy, energy, and sensory perception, but can cause dehydration and paranoia.
